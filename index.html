  • A show about Toronto, 2009

  • A show about queer life before queer life had a language
  • A show about the internet arriving in increments
  • A show about the water rising............

THE WORLD

Toronto in 2009 was one of the most progressive cities in the world. Genuinely multicultural, genuinely queer-friendly. A place where a certain kind of freedom felt not just possible but normal. Inside that freedom, a zennial scene was building a culture and living through addiction, mental illness, abuse, and bigotry without the language to address any of it. They were also gentrifying the working-class neighbourhood they had colonized with cool, without noticing.

The Show

Each season is a single year, beginning in 2009. A different character steps to the center while the ensemble persists, the same world accumulating. Running underneath every season, as constant and indifferent as weather, are the two forces that will eventually end this world: developers moving through the West End the way water moves through wood, and the internet arriving in increments, turning a physical community into a set of images of itself.

The characters don't see it coming. We do.
